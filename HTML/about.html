Biography

  • Name: Jash Vaidya, Age: 21
  • Occupation: Student
  • Base of Operations: Blacksburg, VA (currently)

Jash Vaidya was born in Ahmedabad, India. At the age of 7, he and his family moved to Waco, Texas in 2000. In 2007, they moved to Roanoke, Virginia.

At an early age he developed an interest in video games and technology. Growing up, a big part of his life was on computers discovering what the internet offered and spending endless hours on Runescape. When he was not indoors, he was outside hanging out with his older brother. He discovered his favorite type of music, metal, through one of his favorite games at the time: Guitar Hero 3.

During his last year of high school, he took a programming class and developed an interest in it. After graduation, he attended his local community college Virginia Western and spent two years learning Computer Science. Recently, he transferred to Radford University to complete his Bachelor's in Computer Science: Software Engineering. He plans to become a Software Engineer that would go on to work for a reputable company that could help the world.
